Legal Advisory

Legal Matters

At VGC SRL, we have a team of experts in all branches of law, offering solid and personalized legal solutions. We provide advice and representation in civil, commercial, criminal, administrative, constitutional, real estate matters, and more, ensuring a professional and reliable service for every legal need.

Civil Advisory

At VGC SRL, we provide specialized advice in all areas of civil law, offering efficient solutions to protect the rights and interests of our clients. Our team of professionals has extensive experience in the prevention and resolution of conflicts related to contractual obligations, property, civil liability, family law, and successions.

Commercial Legal Advisory

At VGC SRL, we offer comprehensive commercial legal advisory services aimed at companies, entrepreneurs, and independent professionals. Our mission is to provide solid legal support for the development, protection, and growth of your commercial activities.

Constitutional Law Advisory

At VGC SRL, we provide legal advisory services in constitutional matters, aimed at protecting and guaranteeing the fundamental rights of individuals and organizations. We assist in issues related to respect for constitutional guarantees, defense against acts of authority that violate rights, and the development of legal strategies in amparo proceedings, unconstitutionality actions, and other constitutional control mechanisms. Our commitment is to offer clear and effective solutions to preserve the legal order and constitutional principles in any area where controversies arise or legal intervention is required.

Real Estate Law Advisory

At VGC SRL, we offer comprehensive advice in real estate law, covering the purchase, sale, lease, and regularization of real estate. We provide support in drafting and reviewing contracts, property title studies, establishing guarantees, and resolving conflicts related to the tenure, possession, or ownership of real estate. Our goal is to ensure legal security in every real estate transaction, protecting our clients' interests at all times.

Criminal Law Advisory

At VGC SRL, we provide advice and defense in criminal law matters, whether as a complainant or defendant. We assist in all stages of the criminal process, from filing complaints to representation before judicial authorities. We work on cases of property crimes, crimes against persons, economic crimes, among others, always with the commitment to protect the rights and guarantees of our clients.
